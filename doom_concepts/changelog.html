24 June 2024

  • Added an indicator for new weapons.
  • new weapons : Electro-Tonfa, Classic Pistol, Heavy Pistol, Flame Shot, Charge Shotgun, Mag Shotgun, Bullpup Rifle, Pulse Rifle, Super Machinegun, DPRL, Laser Carbine, Sonic Railgun, Demontech Rifle, Soul Rifle, Devastator, Incendiary Grenade, Micro Missile Swarm, Targetting Beacon, Duel Pistols, Liberator, Fury of the Archangel, Minigun MP5, Dual "Depredations" Machine-pistols, Serious Cannon
  • Renamed the Security Pistol to the Plasma Pistol and changed the description accordingly. Also added a new Security Pistol based on the one from Gunlocker.
  • The 40mm Shotgun now needs to reload.
  • Moved the 40mm Upgrade to the Quad Shotgun and gave the 40mm Shotgun a new Upgrade.
  • Nerfed the Jackhammer Upgrade.
  • The Minigun is no longer a Durability weapon, but now lowers movement speed while firing.
  • Enemies charged by the Upgraded Laser Cannon now also reflect lasers onto other targets.
  • The Upgraded BFG 10000 now consumes less ammo.
  • Added the Demonic weapon category. Demonic weapons are Special weapons using Demon Energy as ammo.
  • Added the Powerup weapon category. Powerup weapons are temporary weapons granted through Weapon Spheres.
  • Added the Disposable weapon category. Disposable weapons are powerful weapons that are discarded after use and can only be refilled by picking up additional copies.
