31 December 2024

  • New ammo type : Spirit. You gain 1 spirit per kill, as long as you have a spirit weapon in your inventory.
  • 20 new weapons : Katana, Heat Injector, Nanotech Shotgun, Covert Rifle, Heavy SMG, LMG, Rotary Chaingun, Assassin Rifle, Ice Thrower, MIZAR, Buzzer SMG, Kyanite Wand, Scarlet Claws, Radiant Tome, Stasis Orb, Love Magic Reactor, Spirit of Damocles, Voodoo Doll, FZRC Plasma Pistol, Soul Cube.
  • 12 new enemies : Kamikaze, Fire Husk, Sentry, Legionary, Daedabus, Mafibus, Corpulent, Tankubus, Fusion Arachnotron, Arachnotron Mk2, Arachknight, Scorpiotron.
  • 4 new items : Spirit Sphere, Armory Pack, Fully Loaded Pack, Motion Tracker.
  • Reworked the GPMG into the MMG.
  • Changed the description of the Plague Shotgun, Minigun, Frostfire Annihilator and Cryo Grenade.
  • Simplified the Double Tap Upgrade.
  • Reworked the BFG 9000 Upgrade.
  • Changed the Frostfire Annihilator Upgrade to alternate between shot types instead of randomly picking between them.
  • Soul Spheres variants now also grant 100 spirit.

15 December 2024

  • Added the Enhanced Powerup weapon category. They are weapons granted through Weapon Spheres after using a Sphere Enhancer on them.
  • Added the Magic weapon category. They are weapons that use regenerating Mana as ammo and can be temporarily powered up with an Ace of Stars.
  • Grouped Contracts and Runes into a new item category called Rare Drops.
  • 20 new weapons : Wands (Amethyst, Topaz, Emerald, Sapphire, Ruby, Diamond, Onyx), Pyro Staff, Icicle Storm, Blood Syphon, Void Crush, Reality Blade, Pulsargun Mark 2, Nemesis Autoshotgun, Dissonance "Expurgation" Machine-pistols, Seriously Big Cannon, Warblade Exoframe, Rainbowmizator, DOOM, Pain Grenades.
  • 14 new items : Minor Spirit Shield, Major Spirit Shield, Reactive Defense Array, Radiance Sphere, Ammo Sphere, Rune of Bulwark, Rune of the Juggernaut, Ace of Hearts, Ace of Diamonds, Ace of Clubs, Ace of Spades, Ace of Stars, Ace of Crowns, Sphere Enhancer.
  • Changed the Eviscerator to be a Partially Disposable weapon.
  • Changed the Vigdal Exoframe to be full-auto, but decreased its firepower per shot.
  • Updated all Buff and Power Spheres with effects for the enhanced versions.
  • Restricted the Rune of Prudence to 1 use per map.
  • Updated the effects of the Contract with Baal to make it more predictable.

22 July 2024

  • New category : Lore. Because some things I want to write about don't fit into the other 3 categories.
  • Removed lore sections about enemies. They are now part of the dedicated lore category.
  • 10 new weapons : Auto Pistol, Leadburster Pistol, Burst Rifle, Xeno Rifle Prototype, Fusion Repeater, Fission Cannon, Demontech Blunderbuss, Demontech Arquebus, Demontech Ballista, Demontech Staff.
  • 16 new enemies : Cyber Imp, Pyro Imp, Devil, Harvester, Blood Fiend, Mech Fiend, Carrion, Mauler, Apparition, Stalker, Phase Spectre, Weeper, Phantom, Terror, Drone, Plasma Soul.
  • 6 new items : Contract with Aamon, Contract with Azazel, Contract with Baal, Contract with Baphomet, Contract with Moloch, Contract with Paimon.
  • Changed the SMG category from Sidearm to Automatic.
  • Changed the Gyrojet Shotgun to the Rocket Swarmer, changed its category to Launcher and increased projectile count to 8.
  • Reduced the ammo consumption of the Soul Caliber to 1.
  • Changed the category of both Annihilator variants to Demonic.
  • Changed a bunch of enemy descriptions.
  • Changed the Flying Mastermind to Sentient.
  • Changed the SS Nazi to Nazi Guard.
  • Increased cost of signing contracts to 20 max health.

16 July 2024

  • Added Contracts. They are rare drops that grant permanent buffs in exchange for some of your vitality. They will get a secondary use later on.
  • Removed leak protection from the Immuno-enhancer Suit.
  • Added "Sphere" to all powerup spheres.
  • 15 new items : Minor Vitality Booster, Major Vitality Booster, Demonic Plate Armor, Terrain Analyzer, Blast Protection Suit, Glory Sphere, Rune of Prudence, Rune of Evasiveness, Rune of Purification, Contract with Beelzebub, Contract with Belphegor, Contract with Leviathan, Contract with Lucifer, Contract with Mammon, Contract with Satan.
  • 12 new enemies : Female Zombie Scientist, Handcannon Zombie, Zombie Rocketeer, HEP Agent, Slug Shotgunner, Assault Shotgunner, Quad Shotgunner, HEP Engineer, Nailgunner, Plasma Gunner, BFG Commando, HEP Vanguard.
  • The Wolfenstein Attack Dog now has lore. Hooray.
  • Fixed subcategory selector for enemies.

08 July 2024

  • Added a couple of notes about armor and enemy categories.
  • 16 new enemies : Zombie Scientist, SSG Zombie, Chaingunner, Dark Imp, Bull Fiend, Delusion, Forgotten One, Carcass, Hectebus, Agathodemon, Hate Elemental, Hell Squire, Afrit, Exile, Flying Mastermind, Attack Dog.

07 July 2024

  • Added sections for items and enemies, about time. (description for items will come later)

03 July 2024

  • All Melee weapons and attacks now deal double damage when under the effects of Berserk, unless stated otherwise.
  • Added the Partially Disposable weapon category. Partially Disposable weapons are identical to Disposable weapons in function, but they come with their own ammo pool.
  • Added an indicator for weapons which have been changed recently.
  • 25 new weapons : Fire Axe, Machete, Hellfire Sword, Laser Pistolette, Classic Shotgun, Plague Shotgun, Shrapnel Shotgun, Flak Cannon, Grenadier Rifle, Seeker Rifle, Auto Railgun, Hellfire Annihilator, Frostfire Annihilator, Bleeder, Pico Obliterator, Dino Hunter, Remote Detonator, Improvised Flamethrower, Antimatter Blade, Pulsargun, Legendary Shotgun, Vigdal Exoframe, Randomizator, Blast Field Generator, Cacobombs
  • Increased ammo consumed per shot by 1 for the Magnum.
  • The Shotgun no longer has horizontal spread now that the Classic Shotgun exists.
  • The Shotgun Upgrade now also increases horizontal spread.
  • Renamed the Soul Rifle to the Soul Caliber.
  • Removed the health gain from the Gore Gun Upgrade.
  • Buffed the Scythe and its Upgrades.
  • Nerfed the Fury of the Archangel to only insta-kill non-bosses, and dealing high damage to bosses.

25 June 2024

  • Fixed code handling new additions.

24 June 2024

  • Added the Demonic weapon category. Demonic weapons are Special weapons using Demon Energy as ammo.
  • Added the Powerup weapon category. Powerup weapons are temporary weapons granted through Weapon Spheres.
  • Added the Disposable weapon category. Disposable weapons are powerful weapons that are discarded after use and can only be refilled by picking up additional copies.
  • Added an indicator for new weapons with the option to only show new additions.
  • 25 new weapons : Electro-Tonfa, Classic Pistol, Heavy Pistol, Security Pistol, Flame Shot, Charge Shotgun, Mag Shotgun, Bullpup Rifle, Pulse Rifle, Super Machinegun, DPRL, Laser Carbine, Sonic Railgun, Demontech Rifle, Soul Rifle, Devastator, Incendiary Grenade, Micro Missile Swarm, Targeting Beacon, Duel Pistols, Liberator, Fury of the Archangel, Minigun MP5, Dual "Depredation" Machine-pistols, Serious Cannon
  • Renamed the old Security Pistol to the Plasma Pistol and changed the description accordingly.
  • The 40mm Shotgun now needs to reload.
  • Moved the 40mm Upgrade to the Quad Shotgun and gave the 40mm Shotgun a new Upgrade.
  • Nerfed the Jackhammer Upgrade.
  • The Minigun is no longer a Durability weapon, but now lowers movement speed while firing.
  • Enemies charged by the Upgraded Laser Cannon now also reflect lasers onto other targets.
  • Changed the Laser Cannon description and gave the old one to the Laser Carbine.
  • The Upgraded BFG 10000 now consumes less ammo.
