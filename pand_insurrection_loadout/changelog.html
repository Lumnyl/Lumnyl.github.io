  • Added the option to stop Durability weapons from being added until there is at least 1 regular weapon per ammo type.
  • Changed how the "Guarantee at least 1 Durability weapon" check is handled.
  • Changed the minimum amount of weapons per loadout to 6 to make my life easier.

18 May 2024

  • Fixed slot 7 weapons being able to roll for Chaos augments.

9 May 2024

  • Finally added the secret weapon to the list. The toggle also extends to all future secret weapons.
  • Durability weapons are now underlined to make them stand out more in case they get other text effects.
  • The Flamethrower is now properly tagged as using a magazine.
  • Removed the rule about upgrading placeholder weapons because honestly who cares.

21 April 2024

  • Updated for Pandemonia v2.5.1. I'm not adding support for the secret weapon yet to avoid spoiling people, at least for now.
  • Fixed Superior descriptions only showing up when hovering over the augment image, now it shows up when hovering the text too.

17 March 2024

  • Added tooltips that appear when hovering over a Superior augment.
  • Slightly changed the color of Precision augments.
