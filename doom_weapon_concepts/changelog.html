24 March 2024

  • 10 new weapons : DNA Blade, Sawn-off, Chromatic Shotgun, Nailgun, Gore Gun, Disintegrator, NUKE, Rolling Thunder, Mini Turret, Scythe.

  • Added the Artifact weapon category and moved the Artifact there.
  • Added missing description to the Crystalline Needler.
  • Miscellaneous tweaks and corrected mistakes.

  • Many description tweaks.
